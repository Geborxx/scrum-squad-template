- Status: proposed
- Priority: high

Goal:

Make the central capability layer explicit in the project structure and documentation.

Expected outcome:

- Identify the first shared specialist capabilities.
- Define what belongs to central capabilities versus team-local roles.
- Clarify escalation boundaries between embedded team roles and central specialists.

Initial candidates:

- UX or UI central capability
- Quality Management central capability
- Platform or DevOps capability
- Security capability
- Data or AI capability
- DBA or Architecture capability

### B-002 Add CoP And CoE Structure

- Status: proposed
- Priority: high

Goal:

Create an explicit model for Communities of Practice and Centers of Excellence across AI Squads.

Expected outcome:

- Define how local specialists share practices across teams.
- Clarify how CoPs and CoEs contribute back to central guidance.
- Make the federated learning loop operational rather than only conceptual.

Initial candidates:

- UX Expert CoP
- Quality Expert CoP
- Future specialist CoPs for platform, security, data, or architecture
